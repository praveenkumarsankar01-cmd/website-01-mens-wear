Shipping Policy

We offer free shipping on all prepaid orders across India. Orders are typically dispatched within 1–2 business days. Standard delivery takes 4–7 business days depending on your location. Expedited shipping options may be available at checkout.


Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via SMS and email. For cash-on-delivery (COD) orders, a nominal shipping fee of ₹49 applies.

Payment Policy

We accept all major payment methods including UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit Cards, and Cash on Delivery (COD). All online transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We do not store any card information on our servers.


WhatsApp-based ordering is also supported — simply select your product, choose your size, and tap "Order on WhatsApp" to place your order directly with our team.

Return & Refund Policy

We offer a 3-day easy replacement policy. If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, you can request a replacement within 3 days of delivery. Please contact us at info@Vynox.com with your order number and photos of the item.


Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. Sale and discounted items are eligible for exchange but not refund.
