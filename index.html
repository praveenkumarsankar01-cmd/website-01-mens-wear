💡 Refund Policy

Vynox currently offers replacement only — we do not process cash refunds. If a replacement is not possible (e.g., item out of stock), we will issue a store credit valid for 6 months. For COD orders, refunds are processed via bank transfer within 5–7 business days after approval.

Replacement Process Timeline

Once your replacement request is approved, the reverse pickup is scheduled within 2 business days. After we receive and inspect the returned item, the replacement is dispatched within 3–5 business days. You will receive a tracking update on WhatsApp.
